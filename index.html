Definition

Founded

April 10, 2015

“

IVSA Mansoura serves as the bridge between local students and the international veterinary community.

The chapter aims to empower students by enhancing their academic and practical skills, promoting communication and exchange of experiences locally and internationally.

Our Vision

As a local chapter of the IVSA, we aim to transform the educational landscape for veterinary students.

01. Global Connectivity

Bridging the gap between local talent and global standards through international exchange.

02. Educational Equity

Creating an inclusive environment where peer-to-peer knowledge sharing is accessible.

Our Mission

We are committed to the development of veterinary education through student-led initiatives.

01. Holistic Training

Supporting non-traditional learning in management and animal welfare.

02. Collaborative Leadership

Promoting the values of teamwork and active community engagement.
